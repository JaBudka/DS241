student_survey {dsbox}R Documentation

Student survey

Description

During the first week of class in Spring 2015, students in an introductory statistics course at Duke University came up with survey questions designed to collect data on variables they are interested in working with throughout the semester. Students answered these questions anonymously in an online survey, and the resulting data are distributed here. Note that these data are somewhat cleaned and potentially identifiable cases are removed.

Usage

student_survey

Format

A tibble with 80 rows and 53 variables:
